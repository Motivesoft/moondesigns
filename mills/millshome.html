Welcome to the Mills Manufacturing Website for 2006

Located in Royal Leamington Spa, we are suppliers of CNC machining centres from some of the world?s leading machine-tool builders: Doosan Daewoo, Kuraki and O-M.

We are committed to supplying the best quality machine tools at the most competitive prices, while also offering unrivalled levels of service and technical support.

Our engineering workshop facility is fitted out with a 16-ton overhead crane and specialist logistics equipment, which means we can handle up to 350 CNC machine tool movements each year.

Our sales centre proudly boasts a permanent display of up to 25 state-of-the-art machine tools from the Mills range, all of which are equipped to run full demonstrations.
